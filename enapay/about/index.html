About Sharon Hartley:

Building Better Futures with Courage & Authenticity

At the heart of enapay Consulting is Sharon Hartley, a leadership coach and business improvement expert with over two decades of experience helping businesses and individuals achieve their full potential. With a career spanning multiple industries—including aerospace, automotive, and defence —Sharon has led major transformation projects, coached and mentored leaders at all levels, and built high-impact development programmes. Her approach is grounded in her core values of Courage, Curiosity, and Authenticity, ensuring that every engagement is meaningful, empowering, and results-driven.

What Drives Me:

I believe that true leadership is not about titles or hierarchy—it's about empowering others, unlocking potential, and creating environments where people can thrive. Whether working with businesses on operational excellence or coaching individuals through personal and professional challenges, my goal is to help others build confidence, resilience, and purpose.

How I Work

At enapay Consulting, I take a practical, people-first approach to business improvement and leadership development.

My work includes:

  • Executive & Leadership Coaching: Helping leaders navigate complexity, build confidence, and achieve their goals.
  • Business Improvement & Change Leadership: Supporting organisations in driving sustainable improvements through people, processes, and systems.
  • Personal & Team Development: Empowering individuals and teams with the tools they need to succeed, using frameworks like Belbin, DiSC, and Inner Development Goals.
  • Emerging Leaders Programmes: Equipping the next generation with the skills, mindset, and confidence to lead.

Why Work with Me?

Because I bring more than just expertise—I bring real-life experience, resilience, and a deep commitment to growth. I have walked the difficult paths, navigated challenges, and learned that success is not just about strategy—it's about mindset, confidence, and authenticity.

Let's work together to create growth, transformation, and impact.

Whether you're looking for coaching, consulting, or leadership development, I'd love to explore how we can collaborate.
