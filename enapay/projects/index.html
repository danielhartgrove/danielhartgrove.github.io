Success Stories

At enapay Consulting, we measure success by the real-world results our clients achieve. From driving operational efficiencies to empowering leaders and teams, our work makes a difference.

Driving Operational Excellence

Problem: A global manufacturing client faced production delays and significant waste in their workflow. Inefficiencies were costing the business over £500K annually.

Solution: enapay Consulting deployed a Lean Six Sigma framework, working closely with cross-functional teams to identify and eliminate process bottlenecks.

Results:

  • Reduced production cycle time by 35%.
  • Realised annual savings of £450K within the first 12 months.
  • Improved employee engagement through inclusive problem-solving workshops.

Enhancing Leadership Effectiveness

Problem: A fast-growing SME lacked structured leadership development, leading to high turnover and inconsistent team performance.

Solution: Delivered a customized “Foundations of Leadership” program, combining group workshops and 1:1 coaching.

Results:

  • Increased employee retention by 20%.
  • Enhanced leadership confidence and decision-making capabilities.
  • Created a unified vision for team management and growth.

Building High Performing Teams

Problem: A corporate client struggled with fragmented communication and low collaboration amongst departments.

Solution: Introduced team assessments using Belbin reports and facilitated workshops to align strengths with organisational goals.

Results:

  • Improved cross-departmental collaboration by 41%.
  • Boosted morale and clarity in role expectations.
  • Fostered a culture of accountability and shared success.
