A bit about us...

We are a sustainable personal care product supplier. Our products care for the environment, are affordable, and lovely to use. Try us and see what you think; you may come back for more.


When we learned that personal care products contribute 20-40% of global plastic waste, we knew change was needed immediately. We're making it effortlessly easy and affordable to make the switch.


Some pretty good reasons for using Reefyll:
  • We use only natural ingredients where possible.
  • Our products are Vegan Friendly.
  • We only use recycled plastic for the reusable bottles.
  • All packaging is recyclable.
  • We offer 90% less carbon emissions than most bottled soap companies.

We're not only removing single use plastic from personal care and making refill & reuse the norm. We're making it more carbon efficient.


Did you know that products such as hand soap and shampoo are made from up to 95% water? That's a lot of unnecessary water being shipped around the world.

By packing the ingredients into our powerful tablets, we've streamlined our packaging. This has resulted in much less transport; one lorry of Reefyll is equivalent to twelve of leading brands.


The result? Our carbon footprint is over 90% lower than competitive brands.
